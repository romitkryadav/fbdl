How We Operate

FBDownloader acts as a bridge between the Facebook video stream and your device. When you provide a link, our system identifies the video source and provides you with direct download links for the available qualities (SD and HD).

We do not host any videos on our servers; instead, we facilitate the direct download from Facebook's CDN (Content Delivery Network) to your local storage. This ensures the highest possible quality and speed.
